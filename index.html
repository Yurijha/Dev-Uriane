Services

Freelance Front-End Development

The development world is full of options for creative, analytical, and securing occupations. I personally enjoy and employ skills for Front End Development. The greatest part of being a programmer or coder is the constant need and ability to learn new technologies. That means there is essentially no limit to the type of work or role a developer can play in a creation process. So while my skills may lean in one direction, I as well as others in this field have the aptitude to apply what we've learned thus far to another role while gaining new information even if it doesn't directly stem from the technologies we're accustomed to.


Front End Devlopment is building out the visual components of a website. I build interactive websites for whatever your specific needs are. This can include if necessary a CMS (Content Management System) for easy client access such as WebFlow, Wix, or Wordpress.


Development beyond coding - While coding is the way to expand a company virtually whether it be with a website or an application, the core process is having space for ideas that can promote change and growth. A recent example of this is a website I developed for a company called Egg Harbor Cafe, which was simple but was only built to demonstrate the proposition of MTM (Multi-Team Membership) within the company itself. So, the website was merely a tool to present the information in an engaging manner so that strategy for a new company team to improve efficiency could grab the audiences attention.
