Services

Website Development

Hand-coding a website from scratch is the most straight-forward approach to constructing a product with your specific needs and business requirements.

CMS Development

Your website can be developed using a CMS (Content Management System) like WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, and Webflow. These systems are user-friendly allowing you to login simply and update content on your website at any time. You can make structural, textual, or graphic changes to the website without much effort or requiring coding knowledge.

Website Design

Simplistic design is the standard but we don't have to stop there. The goal is to create a virtual representation of your company's brand and make sure your audience has ease of use. You can be presented with a variety of design concepts to review in order to get feedback and provide you the chance to express your vision and make modifications before the completed product.

SEO

SEO (Search Engine Opimization) is the practice of improving and promoting a website in order to increase the number of visitors it receives.

Development Beyond Coding

While coding is the way to expand a company virtually whether it be with a website or an application, the core process is having space for ideas that can promote change and growth. A recent example of this is a website I put together for a company called Egg Harbor Cafe, which was simple but was only built to demonstrate the proposition of MTM (Multi-Team Membership) within the company itself. So, the website was merely a tool to present the information in an engaging manner so that strategy for a new company team to improve efficiency could grab the audiences attention. After presenting to corporate members the integration of MTM was approved.
