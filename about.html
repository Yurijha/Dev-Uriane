A Peek Into Who I Am

Hello, my name is Uriane —a creative technologist who bridges the gap between software development and digital marketing. My artistic foundation seamlessly transitioned into the world of coding, where I discovered that building digital experiences is another form of creative expression.​


With a strong background in front-end development, I craft user-centric websites and applications that are both functional and visually compelling. With a robust foundation in front-end development, I specialize in creating responsive, user-focused digital experiences. My expertise spans a range of modern programming languages and frameworks, including:

  • HTML5 & CSS3: Crafting clean, semantic, and standards-compliant code to build visually compelling and accessible interfaces.
  • React: Building modular, reusable components for scalable web applications with enhanced performance and seamless user experiences.
  • Tailwind CSS & Bootstrap: Leveraging utility-first CSS frameworks to streamline development and ensure design consistency across projects.
  • Version Control with Git: Collaborating efficiently on team projects with a strong command of Git workflows, GitHub, and GitLab.
  • API Integration: Connecting applications to external data sources and services using RESTful APIs, enabling enhanced functionality and interoperability.

In addition to these core technical skills, I have experience working with content management systems like WordPress and Shopify, where I customize themes, optimize performance, and implement e-commerce solutions. This hands-on expertise ensures I can deliver tailored solutions for a variety of business needs, from small startups to established enterprises. My coding philosophy combines precision, creativity, and problem-solving, allowing me to adapt to emerging technologies and industry trends. Whether building an intuitive user interface or debugging complex systems, I approach every challenge with meticulous attention to detail and a passion for continuous learning.


Simultaneously, my expertise in digital marketing empowers me to enhance online visibility, engagement, and conversions. I specialize in creating strategic campaigns that combine paid and organic methods to deliver measurable results. My experience includes:

• SEO: Optimizing websites to improve search engine rankings and drive organic traffic.
• Paid Search & Advertising: Managing campaigns across platforms like Google Ads and social media channels, focusing on maximizing ROI and engagement.
• Social Media Strategy: Developing targeted content and campaigns to increase brand awareness and connect with audiences effectively.
• Email Marketing: Crafting impactful email campaigns that nurture leads and drive conversions.
• Analytics & Reporting: Utilizing tools like Google Analytics to track performance metrics, generate actionable insights, and refine strategies for continuous improvement.

By merging technical skills with marketing strategies, I offer a holistic approach to digital solutions that drive growth and innovation. Whether it's optimizing a website for search engines or crafting a multi-channel marketing campaign, my goal is to deliver results that align with your vision and objectives.
