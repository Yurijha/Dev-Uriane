Development Beyond Coding

While coding is the way to expand a company virtually whether it be with a website or an application, the core process is having space for ideas that can promote change and growth. A recent example of this is a website I put together for a company called Egg Harbor Cafe, which was simple but was only built to demonstrate the proposition of MTM (Multi-Team Membership) within the company itself. So, the website was merely a tool to present the information in an engaging manner so that strategy for a new company team to improve efficiency could grab the audiences attention.
